Our Story

Preserving fashion history since 2018.

From a Personal Archive to a Global Movement

What started as a small personal collection in a downtown loft has grown into a premier destination for vintage connoisseurs around the world. At Curated, we don't just sell old clothes; we salvage pieces of history.

Every piece in our shop has been individually sourced, meticulously inspected, and lovingly restored by our team of experts. We believe that true quality never goes out of style.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every garment we curate.

Sustainability

Extending the lifecycle of garments to reduce the fashion industry's carbon footprint.

Authenticity

Rigorous authentication processes to guarantee the era and origin of every piece.

Craftsmanship

Celebrating the superior tailoring and materials of past decades.

Inclusivity

Sourcing a diverse range of styles and sizes so everyone can participate in vintage fashion.
