Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

Yes, our memberships are billed monthly and you can cancel or pause your subscription at any time straight from your dashboard settings.

How does early access work?

You will receive an exclusive password via email or SMS 24 to 48 hours before our weekly drop goes live to the general public.

What is a sourcing request?

Archivist members can submit descriptions or photos of specific vintage "grail" items they are looking for. Our team will put out feelers to our global network of sourcers to find it for you.

How do the styling sessions operate?

It is a 30-minute video call with one of our lead stylists where we review your current wardrobe and suggest vintage additions that fit your aesthetic and sizing perfectly.
